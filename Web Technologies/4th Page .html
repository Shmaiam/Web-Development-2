Favourite Tourist Spots

1. Paris, France

Paris is the capital city of France and is known for its romantic atmosphere, historic landmarks, and world-class cuisine. Visitors can explore the iconic Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and many other attractions.

2. Bali, Indonesia

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its stunning beaches, ancient temples, and lush jungles. Visitors can relax on the beach, explore the temples, go scuba diving or surfing, or indulge in traditional Balinese spa treatments.

3. Santorini, Greece

Santorini is a Greek island known for its picturesque villages, stunning sunsets, and beautiful beaches. Visitors can explore the island's ancient ruins, hike along scenic trails, or take a boat tour to explore the nearby volcanic islands.

4. Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan city located in the Andes Mountains of Peru. Visitors can explore the well-preserved ruins, hike along scenic trails, or take a train ride through the stunning Andean landscape.

5. New York City, USA

New York City is the largest city in the United States and is known for its iconic landmarks, world-class museums, and vibrant nightlife. Visitors can explore the Empire State Building, the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and many other attractions.
