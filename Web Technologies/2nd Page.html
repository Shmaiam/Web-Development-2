My Favorite Personalities

Imran Khan

Imran Khan is the current Prime Minister of Pakistan. He was born in Lahore in 1952. He is a former cricketer who led the Pakistan cricket team to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup. After retiring from cricket, he entered politics and founded the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf party. He has been a vocal critic of corruption and has promised to make Pakistan a more just and prosperous country.

Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and business magnate. He is the CEO of SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and The Boring Company.
